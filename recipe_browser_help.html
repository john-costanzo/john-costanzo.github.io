Recipe Browser Help

  • Recipes Button
    Clicking this will allow you to search through all recipes. Type a word fragment and it will list recipes that contain that word in the ingredients, method or description.
  • Recipes
    • Click on a section header to hide or show the section.
    • Click on an alarm clock icon to hide or show a timer for that particular recipe step. You can start, pause and continue the timer. You can also change the time the timer runs for.
    • Click on an ingredient or a step in a method. This will de-emphasize the ingredient or step to allow you to mark what you've finished.
  • Gear Menu
    • Choose from among small, medium and large font.
    • Print the recipe. (this has known defects)
    • Share a link to a recipe.
